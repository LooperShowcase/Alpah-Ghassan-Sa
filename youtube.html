youtube

Found YouTube was created by three former PayPal employees—Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, and Jawed Karim—in February 2005. The first video, titled "Me at the zoo," was uploaded by Jawed Karim on April 23, 2005s.s Acquisition by Google: In November 2006, Google acquired YouTube for $1.65 billion in stock. The acquisition helped YouTube expand its infrastructure and reach a broader audience. Massive User Base: YouTube has over 2 billion logged-in monthly active users, making it the world's largest video-sharing platform. It is available in over 100 countries and supports more than 80 languages. Hours of Content: Users upload an astonishing amount of content to YouTube. Over 500 hours of video are uploaded to the platform every minute, which translates to around 720,000 hours of new content daily. Global Reach: YouTube has a global audience, with about 80% of its traffic coming from outside the United States. This international reach allows content creators to connect with viewers from around the world. Mobile Dominance: More than 70% of YouTube watch time occurs on mobile devices. The platform offers mobile apps for both iOS and Android, allowing users to access content on the go. Monetization: YouTube provides an opportunity for content creators to monetize their channels through ads, memberships, and merchandise sales. Many successful YouTubers have built lucrative careers through the platform.
